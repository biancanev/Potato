League of Legends Sypnosis

There is no backstory to League of Legends.

League of Legends Rating

League of Legends has some pretty good animations. The only problem with it is that if you don't have good wifi, you might as well just not play, because you will die every time because of the lag. Ther is also the backstory to consider, or the lack thereof. This means that there is no story for you to follow, and it's a bunch of smash and hack, which is fine up to a certain level, but you sometimes want that backstory to see why you're doing all this fighting.
